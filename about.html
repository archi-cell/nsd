About Institute

NSD Computer Institute is a leading provider of computer skills training and tutoring services. We offer comprehensive programs to help students develop essential computer skills and also provide tutoring up to 10th grade.

NSD (National Standard Institute of Information Technology), based in Chandigarh, was established in 2021 to help the nascent IT industry overcome its human resource challenges. The institute focuses on training individuals, enterprises, and institutions.
