About Institute

NSD Computer Institute is a fast growing organization in the field of industrial and vocational Skill training. Since our establishment in 2021, we have been providing quality training to youth, up skill training working youth, women to making them not just technically sound in their chosen field, but also employment ready. The Founder, Navdeep Singh Dadhwal have 20+ years of rich experience in Skill Training with NSDC and different SSC for providing training in IT Sector, Tourism & Hospitality Sector, Telecom Sector, Healthcare Sector for PMKVY /Non PMKVY Schemes . Since our inception, our primary focus has been to equip youth/students with top-notch training. This training not only nurtures their technical skills but also prepares them holistically for the demands of the workforce.
