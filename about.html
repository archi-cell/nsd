About Institute

NSD Computer Institute is a leading provider of computer skills training and tutoring services. We offer comprehensive programs to help students develop essential computer skills and also provide tutoring up to 10th grade. Our experienced instructors are dedicated to empowering students with the knowledge and expertise they need to succeed in the digital age. NSD (National standard Institute of Information Technology) is Chandigarh based, focused on skills and talent developed institute. The Institute was set up in 2021 to help the nascent IT industry overcome its human resource challenges. NSD offers training and development to individuals, enterprises, and institutions.
